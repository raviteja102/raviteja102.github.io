RUDRA is the official team of SRM IST taking part in University Rover Challenge organised by Mars Society since 2013. The annual competition is held at the Mars Desert Research Station (MDRS), Martian Analog Site, near Hanksville, Utah, USA. The team has been successfully operational for the last 7 years and has maintained its consistency in the competition. Our Team consists of 25-30 undergraduate students of every year from various engineering streams, working in different domains.The fun part - R&D, is the soul and essence of Team Rudra.

I worked as Team Leader for RUDRA (SRM Mars Rover Team) for Univiersity Rover Challenge - 2016 Organized by MRDS, UTAH, USA and backed 9th world wide, 2nd Asia, 1st India. 

About the Team

Rudra, the official team of SRM University, has been participating in the University Rover Challenge (URC) since 2013. The team was positioned 5 th across the globe in both 2013 as well as in 2014 edition of the competition and 12 th across the globe in 2015 and 1 st in India. URC, a multi-disciplinary competition, requires the integrating skills of different branches of engineering and science. The team Rudra comprise of 15 members who were divided into four domains of Coding, Electronics, Science and Mechanical. The team emphasises on proper management system and thus follows hierarchy system to ensure higher efficiency of work. The team is constantly guided and supported by Dr. A. Rathinam (Faculty Advisor) and led by S.Balavivek (Team Leader).

About the Competition

The University Rover Challenge (URC) is the world's premier robotics competition for college students.  Held annually in the desert of southern Utah in the United States, URC challenges student teams to design and build the next generation of Mars rovers that will one day work alongside astronauts exploring the Red Planet.
 
URC was launched in 2006, with competitions being held every summer since 2007.  URC consistently draws an international field of the most talented and promising students around. For more information http://urc.marssociety.org/home/about-urc
